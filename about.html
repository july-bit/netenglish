N3T team

N3T is chosen as the name of our group. Actually, it combines the first letters from team member's names. We are the freshmen in IT industry and are currently studying IT at RMIT University. N3T members have different passion such as data, web, game and so on. However, the same point between our team is that we had met difficulties in English before entering international environment. So, it can be one of the reasons which makes us launch NetEnglish site.

Furthermore, N3T looks like NET. This NET word comes from "Networking" and "Internet". Since we are working on the Internet, our project tends to create a large network to connect everyone who has the same passion.
